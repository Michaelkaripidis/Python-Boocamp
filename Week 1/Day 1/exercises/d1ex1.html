About domestic cats

They are often called house cat when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when ther is no need to distinguish them from other fields and felines. They are often valued by humans for companionship.

There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries..

Cat senses

  • They can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for human ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans

The lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests. It is usually more diurnal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at nigth and at twilight. A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in size around composition from three to 20 adults lions, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
